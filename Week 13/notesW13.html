If $f(x,y)$ is continous throughout the rectangular region $R = [a,b] \times [c,d]$ then:
$\begin{align} \iint_{R}{f(x,y) dA} &= \int_{c}^{d}{(\int_{a}^{b}{f(x,y) dx}) dy} \\ &\equiv \int_{a}^{b}{(\int_{c}^{d}{f(x,y) dy}) dx} \end{align}$
